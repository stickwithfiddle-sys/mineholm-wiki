Your First Night

Goal: alive at sunrise. ~8 minute read.

Goal: be safe when the sun sets. One full day-night cycle lasts 20 real minutes. Night is dangerous.

  1. Walk out of the tunnel. That glowing tower is the Worldbeacon — it marks spawn. You can always find home by it.
  2. Check the top-right corner. That is your quest. It will walk you through your first tools step by step.
  3. Find a tree. Hold left-click (or hold your finger) on the trunk until it breaks. Collect 5 wood.
  4. Open the Journal (top-right, or press J) → Craft. Search or pick a category, tap Oakwood Planks, then craft a Crafting Bench. Each recipe card shows exactly what it needs — green when you have it, a how-to-get hint when you don't:
  5. Place the bench: select it in your hotbar, then right-click the ground (or tap PLACE). A ghost outline shows exactly where it will go:
  6. Press E on your bench and craft better tools as the quest asks: stone pickaxe → coal → torches.
  7. Before dark: place torches (they burn bright and scare nothing — but you can SEE), or dig into a hillside and seal the door.
  8. Hungry? Hold food and press E (or tap EAT).
⚠️
At night, Glooms, Skitters and Duskwings hunt. Click a creature to fight back — or run for the light.
💡
Died? Your items are safe in a satchel where you fell. Follow the orange GEAR arrow at the top of your screen to get everything back.

Your First Week

Iron, farms, and a full chest.

Goal: iron gear, a farm, and a chest full of loot.

  1. Mine down. Ores live deep: coal near the surface, then iron, then gold, ruby and crystal near the bottom. Watch for glowing magma on deep cave floors — it burns!
  2. Smelt: craft a Smelter, hold raw ore and press E on it. Iron unlocks the whole mid-game.
  3. Farm: craft a hoe, press E on grass to till it, plant seeds, wait for golden wheat (~2 minutes through 4 stages), harvest with E.
  4. Trees: plant a sapling on dirt or grass. A real oak grows in 4 minutes.
  5. Storage: craft a Chest (8 planks). Walk up and press E when the prompt appears: Use QUICK-STACK in your bag to pour matching items in:
  6. Armor: hides from Woollys and Porkos craft into hide armor; iron comes next. Hold a piece and press E to equip.
  7. Rain happens. Deserts stay dry, snow biomes get snowfall. Weather passes in a few minutes.
  8. Swim carefully: watch the bubbles when your head goes under — surface before they run out.
💡
Full bag while strip-mining? Turn on Junk → coins in Settings: extra dirt and cobble convert to coins instead of being lost.

Claim Your Own World

The Frontier is shared. Yours is forever.

The spawn world (the Frontier) is shared with everyone. Your OWN world is private, permanent, and yours to rule.

  1. Open the Journal → Worlds.
  2. Tap My World → CREATE. Your world is generated with its own unique terrain seed — no two are alike.
  3. Build anything. Everything saves forever, automatically.
  4. Invite friends with the INVITE button — friends can visit, and you can grant build rights (co-builders help quests too).
  5. Set your respawn anywhere: Settings → SET HOME HERE.
  6. Outgrown it? ASCEND preserves your world as a visitable Legacy and hands you a fresh one — with a permanent +25% XP bonus, stacking every time.
ℹ️
Extra world slots (up to 3 worlds) come with the World Slots pass.
ℹ️
Behind the scenes every world has a permanent World Key — its identity. Ascending mints the next generation of the same key, which is why your Legacy worlds stay visitable forever.

Your First Boss

Three totems. Three fights. All drops guaranteed.

Bosses are summoned by crafting a totem and pressing E with it held. They fight where you call them, so pick your arena.

BossTotem recipeHPNotable drops
👑 The Gloom KingCoal ×4 + Glowshard ×2120Gloomstone, Crystal Shard
💎 Crystal ColossusCrystal Shard ×4 + Gold Ingot ×1160Crystal Block, Ruby
🐉 The HydraDragon Scale ×3 + Crystal Shard ×4 + Ruby ×1220Dragon Scale, Ruby, Gloomstone
  1. Prep: iron armor minimum, a stack of food, torches, and an escape plan.
  2. Dragon Scales for the Hydra come from Drakelings — day-time mountain hunters.
  3. Summon with friends: bosses hit hard (6–9 damage per hit) but split attention.
  4. All boss drops are guaranteed — no luck needed.

Enter the Showcase

The weekly build competition.

The Showcase is the weekly build competition. Winning means the whole server walks through YOUR front gate.

  1. Build something worth showing in your world.
  2. Open the Journal → Worlds → Showcase → SUBMIT. Free, instant.
  3. Players tour submitted worlds and vote (1 vote per world per week). Voting for the eventual winner pays YOU 100¢ too.
  4. Weeks end Sunday 00:00 UTC. The champion is crowned at the ceremony, earns 500¢, and their world takes the golden #1 Gate at spawn all week.
💡
RSVP the Sunday “Showcase Ceremony” event on the game page and turn on notifications so you never miss a crowning.

Finding your way

The compass strip at the top of your screen always shows HOME (your spawn or SET HOME point) and, after a death, an orange GEAR arrow pointing to your satchel. Walk toward the arrow to walk toward the thing.

Health, Hunger & Staying Alive

The rules your hearts and drumsticks play by.

You have 20 health (hearts) and 20 hunger (drumsticks). Keep both up and you regenerate; let them fall and things get ugly.

Hunger

  1. Doing things — running, mining, fighting — slowly drains hunger.
  2. Eat to refill: hold food and press E (or tap EAT). See the food table for values.
  3. At 0 hunger you starve: health drains down to 4 HP on Normal difficulty. On Hard, starvation can kill you outright.

Damage & healing

DangerWhat happens
Creatures1–9 damage per hit depending on the creature (armor reduces it)
FallingDamage scales with fall height — water landings are safe
DrowningBubbles drain underwater; at zero you take steady damage
Magma2 damage per second while you stand on it
StarvingHealth drains to 4 HP (Normal) or 0 (Hard)

Health regenerates while your hunger is comfortable. Vitality infusion raises your max health (see Infusion).

Death (it's gentle here)

Your items do NOT scatter or despawn. They wait in a satchel exactly where you fell — follow the orange GEAR arrow at the top of your screen back to it. Satchels persist — even if you log off and come back later.

Biomes & Ores

Where things live and where things hide.

Five biomes, decided by temperature and humidity. Ores are everywhere underground; what changes is the surface, the trees, the weather — and who hunts you.

Gearworks (Machine Logic)

Gearworks is Mineholm's machine logic. Power flows from cores through conduits into things that DO stuff.

PartWhat it does
Crystal CoreThe battery. Powers everything connected to it.
Gear ConduitThe wire. Carries power between parts.
AND Gearbox / OR Gearbox / NOT GearboxLogic! AND needs two powered inputs, OR needs one, NOT inverts. Chain NOTs into blinkers.
Lumen LampLights up when powered — your first circuit.
  1. Starter circuit: place a Crystal Core, run 2–3 Conduits from it, end with a Lumen Lamp. It lights instantly.
  2. Add a NOT gate in the line — the lamp turns OFF (power inverted). Remove it, on again.
  3. A live demo circuit sits on the spawn island — go poke it.

Budget: 500 Gearworks parts per world (the meter shows in your world panel).

Weather, Water & Time

Weather rolls in and out every few minutes. It is biome-aware:

Water & swimming

Dive in — you actually swim. Watch the bubble meter when your head is under: it drains 1 per second, and at zero you take drowning damage. Surface to recover fast. Creatures swim too (and the shipwreck west of spawn hides a chest that takes a dive to open…).

Day & night

A full day is 20 real minutes. Sunrise and sunset glow gold; nights are starry and dangerous. At dawn, hostile creatures stop spawning — but ones already out keep hunting until you deal with them.

Friends, Parties & Referrals

Everything is better co-op.

Visiting & build rights

  1. Anyone you invite can visit your world. Visitors can look but not touch.
  2. Grant Builder to let a friend place and mine in your world. You stay in charge.
  3. Co-Owner is full trust — they can build and manage while you are away.

Parties

Group up with the Roblox party feature (or just join a friend). A party that enters together lands in the leader's world — no coordination dance, no losing each other at spawn.

Co-build quests

Weekly quests reward building together: place blocks in a friend's world, or host friends building in yours (see Progression).

Glossary

Frontier
The shared public spawn world everyone starts in.
Worldbeacon
The glowing spire at spawn. Home landmark.
Showcase
The weekly build competition. Submit, tour, vote — winner takes the golden gate.
Ascension
Retiring your world as a visitable Legacy and starting fresh with a permanent +25% XP bonus.
Gearworks
The machine-logic system: cores, conduits, gates, lamps.
Infusion
Permanent tool/armor power-ups bought with XP + glowshards at an altar.
Blockdex
Your block collection log. Mine a block type once to record it.
Satchel
The safe bag holding your items where you died. Follow the GEAR arrow.
Elite
A rare, stronger creature variant (crowned Mega Blobby) with a coin bounty.
Totem
A craftable item that summons a boss when used (E).
World Key
A world's permanent identity. Ascension mints the next generation of the same key, keeping Legacy worlds visitable.
Block of the Week
The shop's rotating featured block — one themed block per season week.

FAQ

Do my builds save?

Yes — everything in your own world persists forever, automatically. The Frontier saves too (shared).

I can't place a block sideways!

You can — aim at the SIDE face of any block; a green ghost shows where it lands. Blocks must touch an existing block.

Where did my items go when I died?

In a satchel at your death spot. Follow the orange GEAR arrow at the top of the screen.

How do I get iron?

Mine iron ore (needs a stone pickaxe) below the surface, then smelt it: hold the raw ore and press E on a Smelter.

A chest attacked me!

That was a Mimic. Real chests you placed never bite. Unexplained chests in the wild… might.

Why can't I mine the spawn island?

The bastion is protected so it stays beautiful for everyone. Use its stations freely; build anywhere beyond it.

How do the World Gates work?

They teleport you into the current top-3 Showcase worlds. Win the week and the golden gate is yours.

Is anything pay-to-win?

No. Passes are cosmetics, convenience, and extra world slots. Every block, tool, and boss is earnable by playing.
